Plastic Produce Boxes Outperform Cardboard in Every Metric

The produce industry runs on high volume and thin margins. Every box that fails in transit is a claim. Every cardboard box that softens in cold storage is a stacking hazard. Every single-use box is a cost that compounds across millions of cycles per year.

PP corrugated boxes eliminate all three problems. They're 100% waterproof, maintain dimensional integrity in cold storage, and return investment across 50+ reuse cycles. Elipacko manufactures them with UV stabilization for outdoor field use, custom venting patterns for airflow, and any custom size to match your existing packing line and pallet format.
