Frozen Food Requires Packaging Cardboard Can't Provide

Standard cardboard collapses in frozen food environments. Wax cardboard performs better but still fails: the wax coating degrades through repeated freeze-thaw cycling, moisture eventually penetrates, and structural integrity drops precisely when you need it most — during unloading and sortation at ambient temperatures after frozen transport.

PP corrugated boxes are chemically unaffected by freezing. Polypropylene doesn't absorb moisture, doesn't become brittle at -20°C, and maintains full compression strength under pallet loads in cold storage. The same box survives blast freezer, frozen transport, and ambient distribution dock with identical dimensions and no degradation.

Elipacko manufactures frozen food PP corrugated boxes in food-grade polypropylene, any size, with optional drain holes and reinforced base panels for heavy frozen product. Factory-direct pricing, 0% import duty to USA.
