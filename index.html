What Are PP Corrugated Boxes?

PP corrugated (polypropylene hollow-sheet) boxes are the plastic evolution of cardboard corrugated. Two flat PP sheets bonded to internal flutes create a rigid, lightweight structure that is 100% waterproof, weatherproof, and completely unaffected by moisture or humidity.

Unlike cardboard, PP corrugated boxes are reusable for 50+ cycles, pressure washable, food-grade in FDA-compliant grades, and fully recyclable at end of life. They maintain structural integrity in cold chain, frozen storage, and high-humidity environments where cardboard fails.
