PP vs Cardboard for Industrial Use

In industrial environments, cardboard fails fast. PP corrugated industrial boxes:

  • Waterproof — no failure from liquid spills or humidity
  • Impact resistant — flute geometry absorbs drops and knocks
  • Pressure washable — SDS-compatible cleaning between cycles
  • Pest-proof — sealed flute walls, no rodent harborage
  • No compression creep — holds load under long-term racking
