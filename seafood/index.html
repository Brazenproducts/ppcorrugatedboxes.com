PP Corrugated Is the Right Material for Seafood

No other packaging material handles seafood's combination of moisture, ice, brine, and cold temperatures better than polypropylene corrugated. It doesn't absorb water. It doesn't degrade from brine or fish oils. It maintains structural integrity from blast freezer to distribution dock. And it's fully washable — meaning it goes back into service after a simple pressure wash rather than the landfill after a single use.

Elipacko manufactures PP corrugated seafood boxes with configurable drain holes, food-grade PP material, and wall thicknesses from 4mm to 8mm for different load requirements. Custom sizes to match your existing packing line dimensions. 0% import duty to the USA on all Elipacko products.

Seafood Buyer FAQs

What drain hole configurations are available?

Drain holes can be punched in the base, side walls, or both. Standard hole diameters are 8mm, 12mm, and 20mm. Custom patterns are available on bulk orders — provide your drainage requirements and Elipacko will configure accordingly.

Are these certified food-safe?

Yes. Elipacko uses FDA-compliant food-grade polypropylene on all seafood-designated boxes. The material contains no plasticizers, heavy metal stabilizers, or migration-risk additives. Compliant with US, EU, and international food contact standards.

Can I get custom sizes to match my packing line?

Yes — any footprint, any depth. Elipacko manufactures to your exact dimensions. Minimum order quantities apply for fully custom sizes.

What is the 0% USA duty program?

Elipacko products currently qualify for 0% import duty to the USA — a significant landed cost advantage for North American buyers. This is a product classification benefit, not a temporary promotion.
